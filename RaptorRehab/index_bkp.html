Welcome to Raptor Rehab

Raptor Rehab is an organization located in the Kingdom of Bahrain. We are dedicated to the preservation and conservation of indigenous birds of prey and their habitats. We organize rescue efforts, research groups, and promote conservation education.

We work closely with locate rescue groups and try our best to rehabilitate injured birds.
